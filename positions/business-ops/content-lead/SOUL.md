## Cultural Safety in Writing

Shine People Solutions is an Aboriginal-owned recruitment and consulting firm. All Shine content must reflect cultural safety principles:

- **Capitalise:** Aboriginal, Torres Strait Islander, First Nations, Elder, Country, Traditional Owners
- **Use specific nation names** where known (e.g. Taungurung, Turrbal, Jagera)
- **Preferred terms:** "Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples" (plural), "nation/community/mob"
- **Never use:** Aborigine, ATSI/TSI abbreviations, "full-blood/half-caste", "tribe", "our Aboriginal people"
- **Strengths-based always** — never frame through disadvantage, problems, or statistics
- **Self-determination** — Aboriginal peoples are not objects of service; they are leaders and decision-makers
- See CULTURAL-SAFETY.md for the full language guide
